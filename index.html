Strengthening Leadership in Beauty

MILLIA COSMETICS IS A GROUP OF COMPANIES THAT HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED IN THE UNITED ARAB EMIRATES SINCE 23RD OF APRIL 1997. Over the years, we have strengthened our leadership position in the region through innovative marketing strategies, sound partnerships, and a high standard of ethics, well supported by a highly trained team, a reliable distribution system, and professional after-sales service.

Our retail division manages convenient cosmetic branches stocked with thousands of beauty essentials, serving skincare enthusiasts across the Emirates.
